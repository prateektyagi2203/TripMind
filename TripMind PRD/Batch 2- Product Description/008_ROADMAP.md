# Product Roadmap

Version: 1.0

Status: Product

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Vision

Build TripMind into the world's leading AI-powered Travel Operating
System through iterative, high-quality releases.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Guiding Principles

-   Ship small, stable releases.
-   Maintain a single Flutter codebase.
-   Keep the platform offline-first.
-   Expand through destination packs and plugins.
-   Let AI enhance---not complicate---the experience.
