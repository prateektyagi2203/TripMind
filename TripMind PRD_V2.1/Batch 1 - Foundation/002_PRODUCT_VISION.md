# Product Vision

Version: 1.0

Status: Foundation

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Vision Statement

TripMind is an AI-powered Travel Operating System that proactively
assists travelers before, during and after every trip. It combines
planning, execution, memories and intelligent assistance into a single
platform.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Long-Term Vision

TripMind becomes the primary travel application a family uses instead of
switching between multiple apps for maps, taxis, translation,
itineraries, bookings, expenses and travel memories.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Problem Statement

Today's travelers use many disconnected applications:

-   Maps
-   Ride hailing
-   Translation
-   Booking
-   Weather
-   Notes
-   Camera
-   Expenses

TripMind unifies these experiences around a single concept: **the
Trip**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Product Goals

## Before Travel

-   Plan itineraries
-   Import flight and hotel bookings
-   Build packing lists
-   Estimate budget
-   Download destination packs

## During Travel

-   AI concierge
-   Live itinerary
-   Flight monitoring
-   Hotel assistance
-   Taxi comparison
-   Restaurant discovery
-   Camera intelligence
-   Offline translator
-   Expense tracking
-   Family collaboration

## After Travel

-   AI travel journal
-   Smart albums
-   Expense summary
-   Trip replay
-   Share memories
-   Future recommendations

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Target Users

## Primary

Families travelling internationally.

## Secondary

-   Solo travellers
-   Couples
-   Business travellers
-   Small travel groups

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# North Star Experience

A traveler opens TripMind and immediately sees:

-   Today's itinerary
-   Current weather
-   Hotel information
-   Flight status
-   Budget summary
-   Recommended departure time
-   Nearby restaurants
-   Nearby attractions
-   Taxi recommendation

without manually searching.

# Product Pillars

## Trip

Every object belongs to a trip.

## Context

Every recommendation uses current trip context.

## Memory

Trips become searchable memories.

## Intelligence

AI proactively assists users.

## Privacy

Users control all personal data and sharing.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Success Criteria

A successful TripMind experience means:

-   Minimal app switching
-   Reduced travel planning effort
-   Better family coordination
-   Automatic organization of travel information
-   Reliable offline functionality
