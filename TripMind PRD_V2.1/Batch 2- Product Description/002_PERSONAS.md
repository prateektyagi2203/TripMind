# User Personas

Version: 1.0

Status: Product

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Purpose

Define the primary user groups TripMind is designed for. These personas
guide UX, AI behavior, feature prioritization and future roadmap
decisions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Persona 1 -- Family Traveler (Primary)

## Profile

**Name:** Prateek

**Age:** 43

**Occupation:** Product Manager

Travels internationally with spouse and two children.

Usually plans trips several months in advance.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Goals

-   Plan complete family vacations
-   Keep everyone synchronized
-   Minimize travel stress
-   Stay within budget
-   Discover family-friendly attractions
-   Find vegetarian / Indian restaurants
-   Keep travel memories organized

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Pain Points

-   Uses too many travel apps
-   Manually tracks expenses
-   Difficult to coordinate family itinerary
-   Internet availability abroad
-   Searching through emails for bookings

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Desired Outcome

Open one app and manage the entire trip.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Persona 2 -- Couple Explorer

Travels 2--4 times per year.

Priorities:

-   Romantic experiences
-   Restaurants
-   Beaches
-   Photography
-   Hotels

AI should recommend experiences rather than logistics.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Persona 3 -- Solo Traveler

Priorities:

-   Navigation
-   Translation
-   Budget
-   Safety
-   Local transport
-   Offline information

High importance on emergency features.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Persona 4 -- Business Traveler

Priorities:

-   Flights
-   Hotels
-   Expense reports
-   Airport reminders
-   Taxi booking
-   Calendar integration

Requires speed and automation.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Persona 5 -- Senior Traveler

Priorities:

-   Simplicity
-   Large text
-   Voice interaction
-   Emergency contacts
-   Medication reminders
-   Easy navigation

Accessibility is critical.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Shared Needs Across Personas

-   Reliable itinerary
-   Flight awareness
-   Hotel information
-   Offline access
-   Translation
-   Expense visibility
-   Camera document scanning

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# AI Expectations

Every persona expects TripMind to:

-   Understand current trip
-   Remember preferences
-   Reduce manual work
-   Recommend relevant actions
-   Explain recommendations

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Primary MVP Persona

The MVP is optimized for:

International family travelers using Android and iPhone.

Future releases will broaden support for additional travel styles.
